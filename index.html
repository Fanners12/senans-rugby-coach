Attacking Structures
Wide-Wide
9 passes to 10 → 10 flat pass to 12 → 12 draws defender, offloads to 13 → 13 straightens, 11 & 14 on depth. Purpose: stretch defence, create space in midfield.
Phase 1 Territory Overlap
Pod System (3-3-2)
3 pods set across the width. Pod 1 (forwards) carries. 9 feeds Pod 2 (edge). Pod 3 holds width. Multiple carry options to stress drift defence. Backs hold depth to threaten off the pod.
Phase Play Forward Power
Crash Ball + Recycle
12 hits up at gain line. Forwards clear ruck immediately. 9 fires to 10 who has inside runner (13 or 6) and width. Forces defence to commit to ruck before recovering.
Momentum Contact
Maul from Lineout
Catch at #2, drive maul towards line. 8 man at back controls exit. 9 peels or feeds 10 on short ball. Backs align flat and wide. Key: protect ball carrier, keep driving until forced to release.

Defensive Systems
Blitz Defence
Line shoots up off ruck ball. 9 pushes out, 10 leads, backs follow in connected line. Target: get above gain line before ball moves. Risk: space behind if kick goes over. Works when: opponent slow ruck ball.
Drift Defence
Hold line, drift outside on ball movement. 10 marks 10, 12 marks 12, hold width. Force ball to sideline. Works against wide attack. Key: don't leave inside gap — inside shoulder always covered.
Ruck Pressure
7 jackals on all contact. 6 & 8 compete for ball. Aim: slow ruck ball, force turnover or penalty. 9 must slow feed if ruck is messy. Rest of defence keeps shape — don't chase breakdown.
